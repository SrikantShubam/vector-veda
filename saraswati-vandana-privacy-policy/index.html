Saraswati Vandana Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-20 Published by Vector Veda

Vector Veda ("we", "us") operates the Saraswati Vandana app (the "App"). This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Usage data such as screen views, session starts, and feature interactions.
  • Vandana Circle data you provide, including display name, room code, prayer counts, and nudges.
  • Device and app information (device identifiers, OS version, app version, and diagnostics).
  • Local preferences stored on your device (for example, language and reminders).

How we use information

  • Provide core features like Vandana Circle syncing and progress tracking.
  • Improve the App and understand usage via Firebase Analytics.
  • Maintain security and reliability.

Sharing

We share information with service providers that help us operate the App, such as Google Firebase (Analytics and Firestore). We may also share information if required by law.

Data retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes. If you want your Vandana Circle data removed, contact us.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the latest version on this page.

Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact Vector Veda at privacy@vectorveda.com.
